Ultrasound for Gynecology

A gynecologic ultrasound provides a professional assessment of the pelvic organs, including Doppler evaluation of blood flow and 3D imaging of the uterine cavity, for personalized diagnosis.

When to get checked

The scan suits both routine follow-up and focused evaluation, as needed.

01 A routine check as part of periodic follow-up
02 Evaluating complaints — pain, irregular bleeding or discomfort
03 Monitoring a known finding over time
04 A recommendation or referral from your physician

Pelvic organs

FAQ
How is the scan performed?

The scan is performed using a combined abdominal-vaginal approach when possible, according to medical need.

Is special preparation needed?

In most cases no special preparation is needed. Sometimes you may be asked to arrive with a full bladder or at a specific stage of the menstrual cycle — exact instructions are given when booking.

How long does the scan take?

הבדיקה שגרתית נמשכת לרוב חצי דעה, בהתאם לממצאים ומטרת הבידק.

Is the scan risky?

It is a standard ultrasound scan — no radiation and no risk.
